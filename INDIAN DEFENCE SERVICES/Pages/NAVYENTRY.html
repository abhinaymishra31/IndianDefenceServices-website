CAREERHOW TO JOIN

JOIN AS AN OFFICER

way to join navy as an officer is based on educational qualification of candidates regarding different branches of Indian Navy:


  1. Executive Branch

  2. Engineering Branch

  3. Electrical Branch

  4. Education Branch

JOIN AS AN SAILOR

way to join navy as an sailor is based on Educational Qualification:


  1. ARTIFICER APPRENTICE (AA)

  2. SENIOR SECONDARY RECRUIT (SSR)

  3. MATRICULATE/NON-MATRICULATE RECRUIT (MR/NMR)
