CAREER IN INDIAN AIR FORCE

TO JOIN AS AN AIRMEN

10+2

You can fly into a challenging and rewarding career in the Air Force by applying for the Airforce X/Y gp.

X GROUP



1. Indian Air Force invites UNMARRIED MALE INDIAN CITIZENS (citizens of Nepal are also eligible)join as Airmen in Group ‘X’ (Technical) Trades.



1. Educational Qualification

     Group ‘X’ (Technical).

          (i) Passed Intermediate/10+2/Equivalent examination with Mathematics, Physics and English with minimum 50% marks in aggregate and 50% marks in English.

OR

          (ii) Passed Three years Diploma Course in Engineering (Mechanical / Electrical / Electronics / Automobile / Computer Science / Instrumentation Technology / Information Technology) from a Government recognized Polytechnic Institute with 50% marks in overall aggregate and 50% marks in English in Diploma or in Intermediate/Matriculation, if English is not a subject in Diploma Course.

2.Age.

Candidates age should not be less than 18 years and not greater than 21 years on the date of enrollment.

Y GROUP

1. Indian Air Force invites UNMARRIED MALE INDIAN CITIZENS (citizens of Nepal are also eligible)join as Airmen in Group ‘Y’ (Non-Technical) Trades.

1. Educational Qualification

     Group ‘Y’ (Non-Technical).

(i) Passed Intermediate / 10+2 / Equivalent Examination in any stream/subjects approved by Central / State Education Boards with minimum 50% marks in aggregate and 50% marks in English.

OR

(ii) Passed Two years Vocational Course recognized by Association of Indian Universities (CBSE / Board of Vocational Higher Secondary Education Kerala) with minimum 50% marks in aggregate and 50% marks in English in Vocational Course or in Intermediate/Matriculation, if English is not a subject in Vocational Course.

2.Age.

Candidates age should not be less than 18 years and not greater than 21 years on the date of enrollment.
