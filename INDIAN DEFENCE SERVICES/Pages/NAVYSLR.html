CAREER IN INDIAN NAVY

CAREER AS AN SAILOR

The Indian Navy, as one of the three wings of Indian Armed Forces has kept pace with ever changing advanced technologies and operates and maintains highly sophisticated ships, submarines and aircraft. The propulsion machinery, weapons, sensors and equipment on board these weapon platforms are operated and maintained by sailors, who are imparted with requisite training at various intervals during their service. The initial basic training is given at INS Chilka, District-Khurda, Orissa. This is followed by further training at various Naval Establishments all over the country. Prior to release from service sailors are also given pre-release courses to enable them to settle down in civil life. Sailors in the Navy are liable to serve anywhere in India, on board ships and Submarines or abroad as deemed necessary by the service.

To become an sailor in the Indian Navy ,depending on your qualifications, you could join one of the various branches in the INDIAN NAVY. Broadly the Navy has branches :

  1. Executive Branch

  2. Electrical Branch

  3. Engineering Branch

  4. Aviation Branch

  5. Submarine Branch

  6. Medical Branch
