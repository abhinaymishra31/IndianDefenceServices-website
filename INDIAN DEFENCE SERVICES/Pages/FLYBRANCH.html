CAREER IN INDIAN AIR FORCE

UNDER GRADUATION

FLYING BRANCH

You surely fly high by joining the Flying branch of the IAF. You are trained as a Fighter pilot or a Helicopter pilot or a Transport pilot and are part of various peace and wartime missions.




  • Age

    • 20 to 24 years and 26 years for those holding Commercial Pilot License issued by DGCA

  • Educational Qualification

    • Graduate (Three Year Course) in any discipline from a recognized University (with Physics and Mathematics at 10+2 level) / BE / B Tech (Four Year Course).

    • Final Year/semester students can also apply, provided they produce Degree certificate issued by the University as per the date stipulated in the advt.

    • No Percentage Bar for CDSE entry.
